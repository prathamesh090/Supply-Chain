Core Modules

Four Modules. One Pipeline.

Every module feeds the next — demand signals drive inventory decisions, supplier risk adjusts safety stock, and inventory constraints shape the final distribution plan.

📊

AI Demand Forecasting

XGBoost model with 92 engineered features covering lag variables, rolling averages, seasonality, price effects, and promotions. SHAP explainability for every prediction.

98.57% accuracy · Batch 1000 SKUs
⚠️

Supplier Risk Assessment

Weighted composite risk model across 5 dimensions: delivery, defects, compliance, price volatility, and trust. Proactive High/Medium/Low classification before disruptions occur.

ML-assisted · Early warning system
📦

Risk-Aware Inventory Planning

Dynamic safety stock and reorder points that simultaneously integrate demand forecasts and supplier risk scores. Risk multipliers of 1.0×, 1.3×, and 1.6× per risk level.

Dynamic ROP · Real-time status
🚚

Distribution Optimization

Planning-level allocation algorithm that selects optimal warehouse per region by minimizing total cost (transport + risk penalty) while respecting inventory constraints.

Cost-risk optimization · Leaflet maps

The Math Behind the Intelligence

Supplier Risk Scoring

Risk Score =
  0.30 × Delivery Risk
 + 0.25 × Defect Risk
 + 0.20 × Compliance Risk
 + 0.15 × Price Volatility
 + 0.10 × Trust Risk
Category Score Action
● Low Risk < 0.35 Standard
● Medium Risk 0.35–0.65 Monitor
● High Risk > 0.65 Contingency

Risk-Aware Inventory

# Reorder Point
ROP = DDLT + Safety_Stock

# Dynamic Safety Stock
SS = Z × σ_demand
    × √Lead_Time
    × Risk_Multiplier

# Risk Multiplier
Low: 1.0× | Med: 1.3× | High: 1.6×
